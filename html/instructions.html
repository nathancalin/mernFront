How to Play Makimo Sushi Matching Game?

Get ready to sharpen your skills and have fun!

Objective

Match three or more identical sushi pieces in a row or column to clear them from the board. The goal is to achieve the highest score possible by matching sushi pieces before time runs out or moves run out.

Controls

Swipe or Drag: Click and drag a sushi piece to swap it with an adjacent piece.

Match: Align three or more identical sushi pieces to clear them.

Levels & Challenges

Progress through levels with increasing difficulty.

Complete specific objectives like reaching a score, clearing certain sushi types, or finishing within a time limit.

Power-Ups

Use power-ups strategically to clear more sushi pieces, shuffle the board, or gain extra moves.

Scoring

Earn points by matching sushi pieces.

Bonus points for consecutive matches and using special sushi.

Winning

Complete the level objectives to move on to the next level.

The game ends when you either run out of time or complete all levels.

Tips

Look for potential combos and plan your moves ahead.

Use special sushi pieces and power-ups wisely to maximize your score.
